Pygmy Three-Toed Sloth

Bradypus Pygmaeus

Native to the Island of Escudo de Veragua, off the Caribbean coast of Panama.

Fun Facts:

The Pygmy Three-Toed sloth is recorded to be rapidly declining in population from human threats.

This species is the smallest of the three-toed sloths! Most weighing between 5 to 8 pounds.

Although they spend most their times hanging in the mangrove trees, they also appear to be strong swimmers.
