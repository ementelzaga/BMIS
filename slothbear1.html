Sloth Bear

Melursus Ursinus

Natives of South Asia

Fun Facts:

Yes! A Sloth Bear! They are shaggy looking with cream-colored snouts, carrying out mostly normal bear behavior.

The typically feed on smaller insects like termites and ants. Although, they feast on nature's fruits as well.

This species typically has a whitish "V" or "Y" design on their chest.
