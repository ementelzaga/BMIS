Brown-Throated Sloth

Bradypus Variegatus

Found in Central and South America

Fun Facts:

Their slow movement and slow metabolism slows their digestion down to a month for a single meal.

Brown-throated sloths have three toes and three claws on their back feet but only two toes and two claws on their front arms.

They have the ability to rotate their heads up to 300 degrees like owls.
